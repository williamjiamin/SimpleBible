Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app require internet?
Core Bible reading works offline. Some optional features may require internet, including ad loading, in-app purchase validation, and downloading the optional Kokoro Neural Voice model (~600 MB).

Is my data private?
Bookmarks, reading progress, and AI Guide chat content stay on your device. AI Guide does not send prompts or responses to third-party AI services. See the Privacy Policy for third-party ad and diagnostics disclosures.

Does AI Guide work on every device?
AI Guide requires iOS 26+ and Apple Intelligence compatible hardware. On unsupported devices, the feature is unavailable.

What does Premium include?
A one-time purchase that removes all ads and unlocks unlimited AI Guide, Semantic Search, and Kokoro Neural Voice. No subscriptions ‚Äî pay once, keep it forever.

What Bible version is included?
The King James Version (KJV), which is in the public domain worldwide.
